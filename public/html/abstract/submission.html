Abstract Submission Guidelines

  • The abstract should describe original and unpublished research related to conference.
  • All abstracts must be submitted via the congress website ONLY.
  • No edits or corrections will be permitted after the submission deadline. Authors are responsible for ensuring that their abstracts are free of typographical and grammatical errors.
  • The reviewers will judge the abstracts according to scientific, translational or clinical value, relevance to APKASS 2026 Korea & ICKAS 2026, conclusions confirmed by objective results, objectivity of statements, originality of work and overall impression.
  • Abstracts will be published in the congress abstract book only if the presenting author is registered with full payment by the deadline for the registration.

Specific Guidelines for Submission

Notice of Acceptance

  • The presenting author and corresponding author will be notified via the e-mail address provided during submission regarding whether their abstract has been accepted.
  • The notification will include the presentation type and instructions on how to submit the presentation materials.

Conflict of Interest Disclosure

  • If there is the relevant financial activities outside the submitted work, the submitter, who entrust by all of the co-authors, must complete a disclosure form for potential conflict of interest when they submit their abstract. A conflict of interest may exist when an author (or author's institution or employer) has financial or personal relationships or affiliations that could influence (or bias) the author's decisions, work, or abstract. Please find the Conflict of Interest Disclosure Form.

Conflict of Interest Disclosure

  • You can withdraw your abstract in ‘My Page’ before the Abstract Submission deadline. If you would like to withdraw an abstract after the Abstract Submission deadline, please email the Secretariat.
  • All abstract presenters with accepted abstracts are required to complete the registration and pay the applicable registration fee by the specified deadline. Failure to adhere to this requirement will result in the automatic withdrawal of their abstract. The designated date for registration completion will be announced in the acceptance notice.
