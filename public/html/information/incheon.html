About Incheon

Download Guidebook

Located on the northwest coast of South Korea, Incheon is a vibrant port city that blends modern innovation with rich history. As the gateway to Korea, it is home to Incheon International Airport, one of the world's best airports, and offers stunning waterfront views, dynamic cultural districts, and diverse culinary experiences. From the scenic Songdo International City to the historic Chinatown, Incheon welcomes visitors with a perfect mix of tradition and modernity.

Incheon in September

September brings a pleasant change to Incheon, with cooler breezes and clear skies after the summer heat. It’s the perfect time to enjoy seaside walks, explore city parks, and experience local festivals that fill the city with energy and color.
